# Methodology

## Page purpose

Explain the evaluation methodology behind EvalLense.

## User task

Help the user understand that the system does not evaluate decks randomly or based on generic AI impressions.

## Main idea to communicate

EvalLense evaluates pitch decks through structured criteria, judge roles, rubrics, and scoring logic.

## What the page should prove

```text
The evaluation process is repeatable.
The criteria are explicit.
The scoring logic is controlled.
The final result is explainable.
```

## Section structure

### 1. Hero

Purpose:

```text
Position methodology as the foundation of trust.
```

Message:

```text
A structured evaluation methodology for comparing pitch decks with consistent criteria and explainable reasoning.
```

### 2. Why methodology matters

Purpose:

```text
Explain the risk of inconsistent human or generic AI review.
```

Pain points:

```text

Scores drift across batches
Important dimensions can be missed
Generic AI feedback can be inconsistent
```
