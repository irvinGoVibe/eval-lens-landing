# Review Board

## Page purpose

Explain the workspace where teams review evaluated startups, compare results, build shortlists, and make final decisions.

## User task

Help the user understand how EvalLense supports the human decision process after AI-assisted evaluation is complete.

## Main idea to communicate

AI prepares the analysis. The review team compares results, applies judgment, and makes the final decision.

## What Review Board includes

```text
Human-in-the-loop review
Results comparison
Leaderboard
Shortlist decisions
Final decision support
```

Purpose:

