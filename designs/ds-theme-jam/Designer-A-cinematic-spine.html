Why this is one premium page, not a catalog: (1) One anchor — the Lens window — is visible in 4 places and is the literal payload of both branded transitions, so surface changes feel like the camera following an object. (2) Dark is one rising peak (Ink→Nebula→Cinema) framed by two strong branded transitions (Lens Bridge in, Lift Away out), never zebra. (3) Light chapters carry different restrained layers (cool-mist, line-field, concentric-rings, dot-grid, violet-halo) so they're not identical white boxes, but all from one Lens family. (4) Scrub/pin energy builds toward the peak (ring → pipeline → nebula blob → knockout) then releases into calm proof — a real rhythm, not constant motion.
