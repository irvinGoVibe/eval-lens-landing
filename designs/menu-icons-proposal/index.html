Recommendation

Burger → “menu — short” (custom: 3 lines inset to M5–19, 1.5). Reads lighter and more premium than full-width Lucide menu while staying perfectly symmetric and unambiguous.

Close → “× inset” (custom: Lucide x pulled in to 7–17, 1.5). The slightly shorter, optically-centered cross feels calmer and more refined than the full-bleed Lucide x. Both at stroke 1.5 to match the nav icon set.
